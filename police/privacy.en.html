Privacy Policy — MOVTI VPN Shield

Our privacy policy and how we handle user data

Data Collection

MOVTI VPN Shield does not collect any personally identifiable information from its users. The extension accesses public proxy lists and IP checking services to function, but no data is sent to or stored on our servers.

Extension Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Proxy: To modify browser proxy settings and connect to VPN servers
  • Storage: To save connection status and user preferences locally on the device
  • Tabs: To manage browser sessions
  • Alarms: To automatically update the server list

Legal Compliance

Since we do not store any user data, legal requests for personal user information are not possible. All data is stored exclusively on the user's device.
