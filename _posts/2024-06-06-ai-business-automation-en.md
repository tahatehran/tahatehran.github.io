- **Intelligent Chatbots**: 24/7 customer query responses
- **Behavior Analysis**: Understanding purchase patterns and personalized offers
- **Request Classification**: Automatic ticket categorization

### Internal Processes
- **Document Processing**: Information extraction from invoices and contracts

- **Data Analysis**: Automated reporting and interactive dashboards

### Marketing and Sales
- **Content Generation**: Automated article and product description writing
- **Sentiment Analysis**: Understanding customer opinions on social media
- **Demand Forecasting**: Sales and inventory predictions
