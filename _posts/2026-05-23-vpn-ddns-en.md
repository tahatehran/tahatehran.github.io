Secure remote access to home files and services is a fundamental need. In this article, we explore the powerful combination of VPN and DDNS.

## 🌐 What is DDNS?
Dynamic DNS allows you to have a static domain name for your dynamic IP address.

## 🛡 Why VPN?
Using a VPN (like WireGuard or OpenVPN) ensures your security and allows you to safely enter your local network.

This solution is highly practical for sysadmins and power users.
