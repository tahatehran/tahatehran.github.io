Deep Learning uses multi-layer neural networks to model increasingly complex patterns in data.

## 🧠 Neural Network Structure

Artificial neural networks consist of three main components:
- **Input Layer**: Receives initial data
- **Hidden Layers**: Processes features
- **Output Layer**: Produces predictions

## 🔍 Important Architectures

### CNN (Convolutional Neural Networks)
Ideal for image processing:
- Object detection in images
- Medical image analysis
- Robotic vision

### RNN (Recurrent Neural Networks)
Suitable for sequential data:
- Natural Language Processing (NLP)
- Time series analysis
- Text and music generation

### Transformer
Advanced architecture for language processing:
- Large Language Models (LLM)
- Machine translation
- Content generation

## ⚡ Leading Frameworks

- **TensorFlow** and **Keras**: Google's options
- **PyTorch**: Most popular in research
- **JAX**: For advanced computations

## 🎯 Real-world Applications

- Live face recognition in videos
- Intelligent text translation
- Personalized recommendation systems
- Self-driving vehicles
