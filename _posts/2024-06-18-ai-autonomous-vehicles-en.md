Autonomous vehicles combine AI technologies to revolutionize driving.

## 🚗 Key Technologies

### Computer Vision
- **Object Detection**: Identifying pedestrians, lanes, and signs
- **Face Detection**: Driver and pedestrian recognition
- **Object Tracking**: Tracking vehicles and pedestrians
- **Image Analysis**: Understanding 500-meter environment

### Signal Processing
- **GPS Systems**: Precise navigation
- **Laser Sensors**: Distance and obstacle detection
- **Radar**: Speed and position detection
- **Audio Computers**: Important sound detection

### Intelligent Decision Making
- **Path Planning**: Finding optimal routes
- **Emergency Decisions**: Response to sudden situations
- **Behavior Prediction**: Predicting other drivers
- **Speed Control**: Speed adjustment based on conditions

## 🛠️ Development Frameworks

### Operating Systems
- **Linux for Vehicles**: Specialized operating systems
- **ROS for Vehicles**: Robotics framework
- **Autoware**: Open-source autonomous driving

### Learning Models
- **CNN for Images**: Background image processing
- **RNN for Time Data**: Path prediction
- **Reinforcement Learning**: Decision learning

## 🏭 Industrial Applications

### Public Transportation
- **Autonomous Buses**: Urban routes
- **Driverless Metro**: Urban transportation
- **Autonomous Taxis**: Point services
- **Bus Doors**: Students and passengers

### Commercial Transportation
- **Delivery Vans**: Goods delivery
- **Cargo Trucks**: Long-distance freight
- **Drones**: Air transportation
- **Autonomous Ships**: Maritime transport

## 🇮🇷 Iranian Challenges

- **Traffic Conditions**: Heavy and complex traffic
- **Geographic Conditions**: Mountainous and desert roads
- **Transport Laws**: Iranian-specific regulations
- **Cost Optimization**: Development costs
