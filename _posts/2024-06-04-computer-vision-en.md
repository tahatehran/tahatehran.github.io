Computer Vision gives computers the ability to understand, analyze, and interpret digital images.

## 🎯 Main Applications

### Detection and Recognition
- **Face Recognition**: Used in security and authentication
- **Object Detection**: In robotics and autonomous vehicles
- **OCR (Optical Character Recognition)**: Extracting text from images

### Analysis
- **Image Segmentation**: Identifying different parts of an image
- **Object Tracking**: In live video streams
- **Behavior Analysis**: In surveillance systems

## 🛠️ Frameworks and Libraries

- **OpenCV**: Powerful and popular library
- **PIL/Pillow**: Image processing in Python
- **YOLO**: High-speed object detection
- **Detectron2**: Facebook's research platform

## 📊 Key Algorithms

### CNN for Images
- Convolutional neural networks for feature extraction
- EfficientNet and ResNet architectures

### Modern Methods
- **Vision Transformer**: Applying Transformer to images
- **CLIP**: Simultaneous image and text understanding
- **Stable Diffusion**: Text-to-image generation

## 🏭 Industrial Applications

- **Medical**: Tumor detection in medical images
- **Agriculture**: Crop analysis and monitoring
- **Retail**: Product counting and tracking
- **Manufacturing**: Quality control on production lines
