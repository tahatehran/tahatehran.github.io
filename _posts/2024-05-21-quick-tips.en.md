Here are some practical tips to improve your website:

1. Always set accurate **title** and **meta description** for every page.
2. Write appropriate alternative text (alt) for important images to improve SEO and accessibility.
3. Use internal linking between pages to make the site structure clearer for users and Google.
4. Always keep `robots.txt` and `sitemap.xml` files up to date.
5. Write educational content in short, step-by-step, and practical formats for faster consumption.
