Intelligent robotics combines AI and robotic engineering to create robots that can understand and make decisions about their environment.

## 🤖 Intelligent Robots

### Industrial Robots
- **Surgical Robots**: High precision in medical operations
- **Assembly Robots**: Performing dangerous tasks
- **Agricultural Robots**: High-precision farming work
- **Dairy Robots**: Animal care and monitoring

### Service Robots
- **Hospitality Robots**: Service in restaurants
- **Security Robots**: Surveillance and security
- **Educational Robots**: Teaching and training
- **Care Robots**: Elderly care

## 🧠 Key Technologies

### Computer Vision
- **Object Detection**: Environment recognition
- **Smart Navigation**: Finding optimal paths
- **Human Interaction**: Understanding human movements

### Reinforcement Learning
- **Learning from Experience**: Performance improvement over time
- **Access Optimization**: Learning work paths
- **Environment Adaptation**: Adapting to new conditions

### Natural Language Processing
- **Voice Commands**: Voice control
- **Conversational Ability**: Human conversation
- **Request Understanding**: Natural command processing

## 🛠️ Development Frameworks

### ROS (Robot Operating System)
- Popular framework for robot development
- Various libraries for tasks
- Large developer community

### TensorFlow Lite for Robotics
- Lightweight models for low-power devices
- Real-time execution
- Energy optimization
