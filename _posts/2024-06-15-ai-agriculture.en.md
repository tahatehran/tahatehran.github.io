AI in agriculture, known as Smart Farming, plays a significant role in increasing efficiency and reducing farming costs.

## 🌾 Main Applications

### Diagnosis and Diagnostics
- **Plant Disease Detection**: From leaf and fruit images
- **Soil Quality Diagnosis**: Soil data analysis and recommendations
- **Pest Detection**: Identifying pests from images

### Process Optimization
- **Smart Irrigation Systems**: Based on actual plant needs
- **Precision Spraying**: Optimal use of chemicals
- **Smart Fertilization**: Fertilizer distribution based on soil needs

### Prediction and Planning
- **Harvest Prediction**: Optimal harvest timing
- **Market Price Prediction**: Sales decision making
- **Weather Prediction**: Agricultural activity planning

## 🛰️ Used Technologies

### Image Processing
- **Modern Cameras**: High-resolution images
- **Agricultural Drones**: Drone photography
- **Thermal Mapping**: Weather emergency detection

### Smart Sensors
- **Soil Sensors**: Moisture, phosphorus, nitrogen
- **Water Sensors**: Salinity and temperature
- **Weather Sensors**: Humidity, temperature

### Agricultural Robotics
- **Planting Robots**: Automatic seed planting
- **Harvesting Robots**: Crop harvesting
- **Weeding Robots**: Controlling unnecessary weeds

## 📊 Economic Benefits

### Resource Savings
- Up to 30% water consumption reduction
- Up to 25% chemical reduction
- Energy optimization

### Increased Efficiency
- Up to 20% yield increase
- Improved product quality
- Reduced labor costs
