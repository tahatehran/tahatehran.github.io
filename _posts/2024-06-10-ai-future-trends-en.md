AI has undergone remarkable transformations in recent years, and the future is full of new horizons.

## 🔮 Key Trends for 2025

### Multimodal AI
- Combining text, images, audio, and video in one model
- Better understanding of the real world
- New applications in augmented reality

### Small and Efficient AI
- Smaller models with high performance
- Running on mobile and laptop devices
- Lower costs for deployment

### Federated Learning
- Training without data sharing
- Privacy preservation
- Collaboration between servers and devices

- Natural resource management
