Artificial Intelligence (AI) is no longer just a sci-fi concept; it's an integral part of our daily lives and industry. From voice assistants to self-driving cars, AI is redefining the boundaries of knowledge.

## 🤖 What is AI?
AI refers to the simulation of human intelligence processes by computer systems. These processes include learning, reasoning, and self-correction.

## 🌟 Why Now?
With increasing processing power and access to Big Data, Deep Learning algorithms have achieved significant breakthroughs in image recognition, Natural Language Processing (NLP), and complex predictions.

At MovtiGroup, we believe AI is a tool for empowering humans, not replacing them.
