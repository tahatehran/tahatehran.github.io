Natural Language Processing (NLP) is one of the most widely used fields of AI that helps computers understand and generate human text.

## 📝 What Can NLP Do?

- **Sentiment Analysis**: Detecting emotions in text (positive, negative, neutral)
- **Named Entity Recognition**: Identifying names, locations, organizations
- **Text Summarization**: Extracting key points from long documents
- **Machine Translation**: Accurate translation between languages
- **Question Answering**: Intelligent Q&A systems

## 🏗️ Transformer Architecture

The Transformer architecture was introduced in 2017 and revolutionized NLP with the attention mechanism:

### Key Advantages:
- **Parallel Processing**: All words processed simultaneously
- **Long-range Dependencies**: Attention to words across pages
- **High Scalability**: Easily scalable

## 🤖 Large Language Models (LLM)

- **GPT** (Generative Pre-trained Transformer): Text generation
- **BERT**: Text understanding
- **T5**: Translation and task delivery
- **LLaMA**: Open-source models

## 🛠️ Popular Libraries

- **Hugging Face Transformers**: Access to thousands of pre-trained models
- **spaCy**: Industrial-strength NLP
- **NLTK**: Complete research library
