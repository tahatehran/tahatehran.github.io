PostgreSQL is one of the most advanced open-source databases. However, for large projects, it needs precise tuning.

## 🚀 Optimization Tips
- **Shared Buffers:** Set appropriate values for optimal RAM usage.
- **Indexing:** Use B-tree and GIN indexes for fast searches.
- **Connection Pooling:** Use tools like PgBouncer to manage connections.

Ensure your system's stability by following these tips.
