The Continuous Integration (CI) and Continuous Deployment (CD) process is the heartbeat of modern software development.

## ⚡️ Why do we need CI/CD?
Automating tests and deployments ensures the health of the entire system with every small change, allowing you to deploy new code with confidence.

## 🛠 Popular Tools
- **GitHub Actions:** Excellent integration with GitHub.
- **GitLab CI:** A powerful and versatile tool.
- **Jenkins:** The most flexible option for complex projects.
